FAQ

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our premium veterinary services

How do I prepare for my pet's first visit?

Bring any previous medical records and make sure your pet is on a leash or in a carrier. Please arrive 15 minutes early to fill out the necessary forms. Our premium clients receive priority scheduling.

Do you offer emergency services after hours?

Yes! We provide 24/7 emergency support for critical cases. Please call our emergency hotline immediately if your pet needs urgent care. Premium clients receive priority emergency response.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash, GCash, Maya, and all major Credit/Debit cards. We also offer premium installment plans for major surgeries and treatment packages.

Is grooming available at your clinic?

Yes, we offer professional grooming and medicated baths with premium products to keep your pets clean, healthy-looking, and comfortable.
